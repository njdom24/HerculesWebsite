Time Management is Very Important!

There are 7 rules to manage your time properly:

  • Set Correct Goals
    • Know your goals before starting
    • Assign reachable goals
  • Prioritization
    • Set the important stuff first
    • Do tasks in order
  • Time Limits
    • Set a time limit on each task
    • If you don't finish in a certain amount of time, move on and come back to it later!
  • Breaks!
    • Reward yourself with breaks inbetween things
  • Organization
    • Organize your things well
    • It'll help when you try to search for things
  • Remove the Unnecessary!
    • Throw the unnecesary tasks away
    • Save them for when you have free time!
  • Plan Ahead!
    • Plan things in your calendar
    • Try to have a weekly checklist!
